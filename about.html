Personal Biography

Hello! I'm Ayesha Anand, a Year 11 student at Kings Interhigh and a proud member of Jenner House. Born with a passion for bringing people together and making positive change, I've always believed that the best communities are built on trust, kindness, and shared goals.

As an online learner, I understand the unique challenges and opportunities we face in our digital school environment. I've experienced firsthand how important it is to feel connected and supported, even when we're learning from different corners of the world.

My Leadership Journey

Year 9-10

House Captain Assistant: Helped organize virtual house events and supported younger students with their transition to online learning. Assisted in coordinating inter-house competitions and maintained house spirit through creative online initiatives.

Year 10

Peer Mentor: Provided guidance to Year 7 and 8 students, helping them navigate the Kings Interhigh platform and build confidence in their virtual learning environment. Created helpful guides and hosted weekly Q&A sessions.

Year 11

Student Voice Representative: Currently representing student concerns to teachers and administration, advocating for improved mental health support and more engaging virtual activities.

KS4 Student Council Responsibilities

As your KS4 Student Council representative, I will take on these key responsibilities:

  • Student Voice Advocacy: Regularly collect and present student feedback to school leadership on academic and pastoral matters
  • Academic Support Initiatives: Work with teachers to develop resources and programs that support GCSE preparation and study skills
  • Mental Health & Wellbeing: Champion initiatives that promote student mental health and create supportive online communities
  • House Pride & Unity: Organize inter-house competitions and events that build school spirit across all year groups
  • Communication Bridge: Serve as the primary liaison between KS4 students and school administration
  • Policy Development: Participate in creating and reviewing school policies that affect student experience
  • Event Planning: Organize virtual social events, study groups, and celebration activities for achievements
